The Studio

A well equipped studio

Ancient Rose Studios consists of a live room, a control room and a vocal booth.

We're currently renovating to put in a floating floor in the live room - that will give a great sound and soundproofing. We're still recording while that's happening but we'll be back in full force in summer/autumn 2022.
